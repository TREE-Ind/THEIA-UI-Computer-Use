# Shotcut: add a new audio track

Session-verified workflow for adding an audio track in Shotcut using the Windows computer-use tools.

## Verified steps
1. Open Shotcut from Windows Search if it is not already running.
2. Focus the Shotcut window.
3. If the timeline panel is visible, prefer its lower-left hamburger/menu button over the top toolbar `Timeline` button.
4. Open `Track Operations` in that timeline-panel menu.
5. Choose `Add Audio Track`.
6. Re-capture the screen and confirm the new audio track appears in the timeline.

## Useful grounding notes
- Shotcut has both a top toolbar/menu item labeled `Timeline` and a separate timeline-panel menu button at the lower-left of the timeline panel. For adding tracks, the lower-left timeline-panel menu is the reliable target.
- The `Track Operations` submenu opens to the right of the lower-left timeline menu. `Add Audio Track` is the first item in that submenu and may show shortcut `Ctrl+U`.
- Verified visual evidence for success: the window title changes to `Untitled* - Shotcut`, and the timeline shows an audio row labeled `A1` with lock/speaker controls on the left.
- Always verify after the click; do not assume the track was created until the timeline visibly changes.
